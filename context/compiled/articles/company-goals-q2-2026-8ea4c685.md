# Company Goals Q2 2026

The company sets three objectives for Q2 2026: internal general availability for [[Scout v3]], faster new-hire onboarding, and a 30% reduction in customer-reported bugs versus Q1. Each key result is measurable and assigned to a single owner.

## Scout v3 internal GA

Objective O1 is to ship [[Scout v3]] to internal GA.

### Key results

- 80% of engineering has Scout integrated into daily work by the end of May. Owner: Taylor.
- The wiki compile pipeline runs under 60 seconds p95 on a 1,000-file corpus. Owner: Morgan.
- Zero gating escapes: Navigator never reads `local:raw`. Owner: Priya.

## Onboarding time to first PR

Objective O2 is to reduce onboarding time to first pull request from 5 days to 2.

### Key results

- Rewrite the onboarding checklist into a wiki-first flow. Owner: Dana.
- Assign every new hire an onboarding buddy by day 1. Owner: Dana.
- Publish a ramp-up task bank for each team. Owner: team leads.

## Customer-reported bug reduction

Objective O3 is to cut customer-reported bugs by 30% compared with Q1.

### Key results

- Add API contract tests for all public endpoints. Owner: Sam.
- Complete incident postmortems within 5 business days. Owner: Ivy.
- Launch a flaky-test quarantine dashboard. Owner: Jordan.

## Cadence

Status checks happen Thursdays at 3pm in `#eng-status`.
