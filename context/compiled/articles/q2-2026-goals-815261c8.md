# Q2 2026 Goals

The company sets three objectives for Q2 2026: ship [[Scout v3]] to internal GA, reduce onboarding time to first PR from 5 days to 2, and cut customer-reported bugs by 30% versus Q1. Each key result is measurable and has a single owner.

## Objective 1: Scout v3 internal GA

The quarter's first objective is internal general availability for [[Scout v3]].

### Key results

- 80% of engineering uses Scout in their daily workflow by the end of May. Owner: Taylor.
- The wiki compile pipeline runs under 60 seconds p95 on a 1,000-file corpus. Owner: Morgan.
- Zero gating escapes: Navigator never reads `local:raw`. Owner: Priya.

## Objective 2: Faster onboarding

The second objective is to reduce time to first PR from 5 days to 2 through changes to [[Onboarding]].

### Key results

- Rewrite the onboarding checklist into a wiki-first flow. Owner: Dana.
- Assign every new hire an onboarding buddy by day 1. Owner: Dana.
- Publish a ramp-up task bank for each team. Owner: team leads.

## Objective 3: Fewer customer-reported bugs

The third objective is a 30% reduction in customer-reported bugs relative to Q1, centered on quality controls and incident follow-up.

### Key results

- Add API contract tests for all public endpoints. Owner: Sam.
- Complete incident postmortems within 5 business days. Owner: Ivy.
- Launch a flaky-test quarantine dashboard. Owner: Jordan.
