# Company Goals Q2 2026

Q2 2026 has three company-level objectives: ship [[Scout v3]] to internal GA, reduce onboarding time to first PR from 5 days to 2, and cut customer-reported bugs by 30% versus Q1. Each key result is measurable and has a named owner.

## Ship Scout v3 to internal GA

This objective focuses on product adoption, performance, and safety boundaries for internal release.

### Key results

- 80% of engineering has Scout wired into their daily flow by end of May. Owner: Taylor.
- Wiki compile pipeline runs under 60 seconds p95 over a 1,000-file corpus. Owner: Morgan.
- Zero gating escapes: Navigator never reads `local:raw`. Owner: Priya.

## Onboarding time to first PR

The target is to reduce time to first PR from 5 days to 2 days by improving the [[Onboarding]] process.

### Key results

- Rewrite the onboarding checklist into a wiki-first flow. Owner: Dana.
- Pair every new hire with an onboarding buddy by day 1. Owner: Dana.
- Publish a ramp-up task bank per team. Owner: team leads.

## Customer-reported bug reduction

The target is to cut customer-reported bugs by 30% compared with Q1. The work centers on test coverage, incident follow-through, and flaky-test visibility.

### Key results

- API contract tests on all public endpoints. Owner: Sam.
- Incident postmortems completed within 5 business days. Owner: Ivy.
- Flaky-test quarantine dashboard live. Owner: Jordan.
