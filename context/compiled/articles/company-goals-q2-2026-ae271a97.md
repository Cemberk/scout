# Company Goals Q2 2026

Q2 2026 has three company objectives. Status checks run Thursdays at 3pm in `#eng-status`.

## Objectives

### Scout v3 internal GA
- 80% of engineering uses Scout in daily flow by end of May. Owner: Taylor.
- Wiki compile pipeline runs under 60s p95 on a 1,000-file corpus. Owner: Morgan.
- Zero gating escapes: Navigator never reads `local:raw`. Owner: Priya.

### Onboarding speed
Reduce time to first PR from 5 days to 2.
- Rewrite onboarding checklist into a wiki-first flow. Owner: Dana.
- Assign every new hire an onboarding buddy by day 1. Owner: Dana.
- Publish a ramp-up task bank for each team. Owner: team leads.

### Customer-reported bugs
Cut customer-reported bugs by 30% versus Q1.
- Add API contract tests for all public endpoints. Owner: Sam.
- Complete incident postmortems within 5 business days. Owner: Ivy.
- Launch a flaky-test quarantine dashboard. Owner: Jordan.
