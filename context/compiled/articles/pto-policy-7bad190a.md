# PTO Policy

Full-time ACME employees receive 25 days of paid time off per calendar year, plus 11 [[public-holidays]]. PTO accrues at 2.08 days per month and rolls over up to 5 days into the following year.

## Eligibility

All full-time employees are eligible from their first day. Contractors and part-time employees follow [[contractor-leave]].

## Requesting time off

- Submit requests in Workday.
- Give at least 2 weeks' notice for absences of 3 days or more.
- Shorter absences require 48 hours' notice.
