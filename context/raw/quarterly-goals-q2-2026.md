# Q2 2026 Company Goals

Three company-level objectives for the quarter. Each objective has a
small number of key results — concrete, measurable, owned by one
person.

## O1: Ship Scout v3 to internal GA

- **KR1**: 80% of engineering has Scout wired into their daily flow by
  end of May. (Owner: Taylor)
- **KR2**: Wiki compile pipeline runs under 60s p95 over a 1k-file
  corpus. (Owner: Morgan)
- **KR3**: Zero gating escapes — Navigator never reads `local:raw`. (Owner: Priya)

## O2: Reduce onboarding time to first PR from 5 days to 2

- **KR1**: Rewrite the onboarding checklist into a wiki-first flow.
  (Owner: Dana)
- **KR2**: Pair every new hire with an onboarding buddy by day 1.
  (Owner: Dana)
- **KR3**: Publish ramp-up task bank per team. (Owner: team leads)

## O3: Cut customer-reported bugs by 30% vs Q1

- **KR1**: API contract tests on all public endpoints. (Owner: Sam)
- **KR2**: Incident postmortems within 5 business days. (Owner: Ivy)
- **KR3**: Flaky-test quarantine dashboard live. (Owner: Jordan)

Status checks are Thursdays at 3pm in `#eng-status`.
